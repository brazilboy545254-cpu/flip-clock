About Us

Flip Clock is a free online tool that provides a clean and simple digital flip clock and stopwatch experience for users worldwide.

Our goal is to offer a fast, responsive, and easy-to-use time utility that works across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Whether you need a fullscreen clock for presentations, studying, work, or personal use, Flip Clock is designed to be accessible and easy to use.

We continuously work to improve performance, usability, and user experience.
